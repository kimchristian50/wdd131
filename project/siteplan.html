Site Name: Etherdox Photography

  • This is the business name I used for many years, from 2005 - 2021. My husband and I chose the domain name 'etherdox.com' even before that, back in 2001, because it sounded "kind of techy" and we've used it for a couple of things since then, but mostly my photography business. I do still have the domain.
  • The purpose of this project is to revise my old, very dated Etherdox Photography website with something that is designed for mobile viewing and has responsive features.
  • My old logo is above - I think I will keep using it.

Site Scope + Purpose

  • This site provides information for my photography business, focusing on family photography and graduate photography.
  • It describes a photo session and what it costs.
  • It answers common questions about family and graduate photography.
  • It has a form to request more information.

Scenarios

  • What are your family photography sessions like?
  • How much does a family photo session cost and what products would I get?
  • How can I make sure my family is happy and looks nice for photos?
  • What are your graduating senior photo sessions like?
  • How much does a senior photo session cost and what products would I get?
  • How can I set up a session?

Color Schema

  • All the photographers' websites in my city are white with black type. My old site is black with white type. I don't want to do either one.
  • As seen on this page, I've chosen an ivory background.
  • My main font color will be a dark navy.
  • Headings are a slightly lighter navy.
  • Borders and some accents will be a lighter dusty blue color.
  • Hopefully this blue color set is kind of serene and will also set me apart from the rest of the black & white websites.

Typography

  • I've chosen the Montserrat font, as seen on this page.
  • I will probably stick to just one font.
